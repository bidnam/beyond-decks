The constraint

Decks were the highest-leverage format available
and for good reason.

Building things that could travel with clients required developers, budgets, months. Decks were all the economics supported.

The shift

Building is now
nearly free.

The same hours spent perfecting a deck
can now produce a working prototype.

"But we're not developers."

The barrier isn't skillit's imagination.
